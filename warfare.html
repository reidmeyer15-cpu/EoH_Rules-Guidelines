What Makes WARFARE

A complete production designed for total immersion. These aren't bullet points — they're how every WARFARE session actually feels.

Immersive Full-Scale Production
Every WARFARE is crafted around a specific era, bringing its conflict to life through authentic weapons, vehicles, gear, environments, and atmosphere. Not a universal mode — a carefully designed experience built for total immersion.
Dynamic Battlefield Systems
No two sessions play the same. Objectives, enemy activity, encounters, and battlefield events dynamically shift, creating unpredictable combat scenarios that keep every operation fresh.
Major & Minor Capture Points
WARFARE revives the classic Conflict experience with heavily contested Major Capture Points serving as the key to victory. Advance through connected Minor Capture Points to establish forward momentum and secure the battlefield.
Combat Objective Zones
Take on optional Combat Operations across the map with a small team to infiltrate enemy territory, complete randomized objectives, and earn valuable rewards for your efforts.
The Fight Between Objectives
The battlefield is never safe. Between capture points, expect enemy patrols, ambushes, counterattacks, and unexpected encounters that turn every movement into a mission of its own.
Immersive Battlefield Events
Built-in ambient combat systems — mortar strikes, minefields, ambushes, and era-specific battlefield threats that add another layer of tension and realism to every engagement.
Advanced AI Behaviors (CRX)
Powered by CRX, WARFARE introduces enhanced AI behaviors designed to create more aggressive, tactical, and unpredictable enemy forces that operate far beyond standard Vanilla AI.
Enemy Vehicles & Patrols
Ambient enemy vehicle patrols, counters, and systems that engage more than just boots on the ground. Enemies push objectives in vehicles, patrol streets, and reinforce themselves with troop transports.
A Tactical Team Experience
WARFARE rewards teamwork, coordination, and communication. Whether operating in a small squad or a larger force, success depends on planning, cooperation, and tactical execution under pressure.
