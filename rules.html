Before You Read

The offenses listed below are examples — not an exhaustive list of every possible scenario. Every situation is different, and GMs use their judgment to determine the appropriate response. Something not listed here can still result in action if a GM determines it warrants one.

If you are banned, you are entitled to know why. Your ban reason may list the specific rule section — for example, "§ 0.1 Admin Discretion" or "§ 3.1 Mass Teamkilling" — but admins are not required to include the specific subsection. If the reason given isn't enough detail, you can request the admin's full note through the appeal process in Discord and staff will provide it.

If you believe your ban was unjust, you can submit an appeal through our appeal process in Discord. If you're reading this and thinking "how do I get around these?" — you're already the problem.
