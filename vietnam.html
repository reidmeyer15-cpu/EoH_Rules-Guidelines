Vietnam

Dense jungle. Huey gunships. Napalm runs. Our flagship Vietnam servers drop you into the fight with full PvE gameplay across three servers, each with its own intensity level.

Live — 3 Servers Online
Gameplay Overview
PvE Cooperative
All servers are PvE only. You play as the US faction against AI-controlled NVA forces. The only time you'll face player-controlled enemies is during special admin-run events.
Jungle Terrain
Fight through dense jungle, rice paddies, and fortified NVA compounds. Visibility is low, flanks are everywhere, and the terrain itself is a weapon.
Air Support
Huey transports, armed gunships, medevac, and CAS. Air assets are limited to 7 total — 3 transport, 2 armed, 1 medevac, 1 gunship — to keep gameplay balanced.
Open Roles
Nothing is locked. Infantry, medic, pilot, door gunner — play whatever you can get your hands on. No rank requirements, no applications. Just get in and play.
GM Enhanced
Admins regularly enhance gameplay using Game Master — spawning air threats, setting ambushes, and creating dynamic scenarios the AI can't do on its own.
Community Mods
Our servers run a mix of in-house and community-made mods. Everything is hand-picked and configured. Mods download automatically when you join.

Vietnam Pub 2
Typically 50–90 players during the week, max on weekends. Hosts more organized players and is more challenging due to the lower headcount. A good middle ground.

Vietnam Pub 3
The hardcore server. Low player count means less help, less CAS, and a much harder grind. Also our event server — PTSD mode, special ops, and more. Check #role-selection in Discord for event notifications.
