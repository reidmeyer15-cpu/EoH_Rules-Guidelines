Welcome to the Fight

A no-nonsense Arma Reforger community built on good gameplay, solid teamwork, and respect. Whether you're a seasoned operator or just dropping in for the first time — grab your kit and fall in.

How to Join

Open Arma Reforger
Launch the game and head to the server browser.
Find Our Servers
Search for servers tagged ![EoH]. We run 3 Vietnam servers (2 Legacy + 1 Warfare), 2 Takistan GWOT servers, and 2 WW2 Normandy servers — all PvE. Watch out for fakes — some servers pretend to be us. If it doesn't have the ![EoH] tag, it's not us.
Click Join — Mods Download Automatically
Hit join and our mod collection will start downloading. Sit tight — first time takes a few minutes depending on your connection.
Get In, Read the Rules
Once mods are done you're in. Read the rules — ignorance isn't an excuse. If you didn't grab the Discord link from here, it's also on a big board at MOB in-game.
⚠ FAKE SERVERS EXIST — Only join servers with the ![EoH] tag. If someone tells you to join a different server claiming to be EoH, they're lying.
⚙ COMMUNITY MODS — Our servers use a mix of in-house and community-made mods. Not everything is built by us, but we hand-pick and configure every mod in our collection. We do the best we can with the staff we have.
