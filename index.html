Before You Read

The offenses listed below are examples — not an exhaustive list of every possible scenario. Every situation is different, and GMs use their judgment to determine the appropriate response. Something not listed here can still get you banned if a GM determines it warrants one.

The punishment levels shown are the most common minimum for each type of offense. The maximum for any offense can be a permanent ban at staff discretion. Being receptive and respectful when staff talks to you goes a long way. Being a dick about it makes it worse.

Staff reserves the right to skip levels and go straight to a ban if the situation calls for it. If you believe your ban was unjust, you can submit an appeal through our appeal process in Discord. If you're reading this and thinking "how do I get around these?" — you're already the problem.

How Escalation Works: Every offense flows from least to greatest. If you're receptive when staff talks to you, you'll generally get the listed punishment. If you're confrontational, rude, or aggressive during the process, the punishment automatically bumps up one level. Repeat offenses also escalate. Don't make it worse for yourself.
